Date Format

%Y - Year (ex 1988)
%M - Month as xx (ex 01)
%n - Month as x (ex 1)
%Q - Month as its name (ex October)
%q - Month as its abbreviated name (ex Oct)
%d - Day as xx (ex 01)
%D - Day as x (ex 1)
%H - 24 Hour as xx (ex 23)
%I - 12 Hour as xx (ex 12)
%p - am/pm (ex am)
%P - AM/PM (ex AM)
%m - Minutes as xx (ex 05)
%s - Seconds as xx (ex 06)
%r - Milliseconds as xxx (ex 001)
%z - UTC timestamp (ex 594014400000)
